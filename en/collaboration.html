PBL with UCL

TOP SE students have annually Project-Based Learning (PBL) project with students at UCL in England (University College London)

Cooperation with Graduate Schools

We TOP SE collaborate with The University of Electro-Communications Graduate school, Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology(JAIST) and Institute of Information Security(IISEC).

With UEC, we established a cultivation of human course in 2012, in which is for TOP SE students (include alumni / alumnae ) who are motivated to enter Ph.D course.

With JAIST, we held Ph.D course for worker at Shinagawa. Details

At IISEC, you could apply your credits at TOPSE for graduating IISEC. Feel free to ask your questions from here.
